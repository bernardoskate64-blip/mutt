Rolling is a DJ and producer originally from the city of Bariloche who is currently based in Buenos Aires. While his main genre is tech house, a core element of his sonic identity is versatility. Never confined to a single genre, his sound consistently maintains a refined and modern essence. He is the founder and creator of the well-known event brand Livit, which has successfully hosted multiple editions in both Buenos Aires and Bariloche. Rolling has performed in some of Argentina’s most respected venues, including Crobar, The Bow, Morocco, Bypass, Chucaro, Lignee, Apres, among others. He has also performed in Europe at venues such as Bonafe (Ibiza), Mint (Barcelona), Supermercat (Barcelona), and Renate (Berlin). Throughout his career, he has shared the booth with artists such as Rafa Barrios, Chapa & Castelo, Bob Tosh, Dont Blink, Silvestre, Stjefanek, Emi Demarco, This & That, Fab Massimo, Esther Broncal, Franco BA, Martin Huergo, Valdovinos, Super, Raiann, Cordoba, Inda Jani, Luana, Anita B Queen, Matt, among others.
